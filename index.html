二、5–10分钟小测

1. A speaker who uses few words but communicates clearly is ____.

  1. conciliatory
  2. concise
  3. compliant
  4. congenial
显示答案

B. concise

2. If two people secretly plan something dishonest together, they ____.

  1. compress
  2. concede
  3. connive
  4. compliment
显示答案

C. connive

3. A person who is involved in a wrongdoing can be called ____.

  1. complicit
  2. concise
  3. comprehensive
  4. conformist
显示答案

A. complicit

4. A large fire that spreads widely is a ____.

  1. confluence
  2. confection
  3. conflagration
  4. condolence
显示答案

C. conflagration

5. A person trusted with private information is a ____.

  1. confidant
  2. conduit
  3. congregation
  4. concord
显示答案

A. confidant

6. A plan designed to calm conflict may be described as ____.

  1. conciliatory
  2. concomitant
  3. conformist
  4. complicit
显示答案

A. conciliatory

7. When many factors come together at once, there is a ____ of causes.

  1. consensus
  2. confluence
  3. compunction
  4. concord
显示答案

B. confluence

8. If a liquid thickens and becomes more solid, it begins to ____.

  1. congeal
  2. concede
  3. consecrate
  4. concoct
显示答案

A. congeal

四、阅读找词任务

After the storm damaged several homes, the town council met in hopes of reaching concord. One council member gave a concise summary of the damage, while another tried to concoct excuses for why emergency supplies had not arrived. Fortunately, a more conciliatory speaker helped calm the room. Soon, a consensus began to form, and volunteers from the local congregation offered help. Their efforts, combined with donations flowing through a temporary conduit, prevented the crisis from turning into a wider conflagration of anger and blame.

显示答案

consensus
